Success Stories

Celebrating the Achievements of Our Students and Alumni

At Kapadia High School, we take immense pride in the accomplishments of our students and alumni. Their success stories reflect our commitment to providing quality education and nurturing well-rounded individuals who excel in various fields.

From academic achievements to professional accomplishments, our students continue to make their mark in the world, embodying the values and skills they developed during their time at Kapadia High School.

Alumni Spotlight

Our graduates have gone on to achieve remarkable success in various fields.

Dr. Vikram Patel

Class of 2005
Neurosurgeon at Apollo Hospitals

Dr. Patel graduated from Kapadia High School in 2005 and went on to pursue medicine at AIIMS, Delhi. After completing his specialization in neurosurgery, he now serves as a leading neurosurgeon at Apollo Hospitals.

The foundation of critical thinking and perseverance I developed at Kapadia High School has been instrumental in my medical career.

Priya Mehta

Class of 2008
Tech Entrepreneur & CEO

After graduating from Kapadia High School, Priya pursued Computer Science at IIT Bombay. She later founded her own tech startup which has now grown into a successful company with international clients.

My teachers at Kapadia encouraged me to think innovatively and pursue my passion for technology.

Rahul Sharma

Class of 2010
International Civil Servant at UN

Rahul graduated from Kapadia High School and went on to study International Relations at JNU, Delhi. After completing his Master's from LSE, he now works with the United Nations on sustainable development projects.

The diverse cultural exposure and emphasis on social responsibility at Kapadia shaped my worldview and career path.

Anjali Desai

Class of 2012
Award-winning Scientist

Anjali's passion for science was nurtured at Kapadia High School. She pursued research in Biotechnology and has received national recognition for her work on sustainable agricultural practices.

The hands-on science labs and encouraging teachers at Kapadia sparked my curiosity and love for scientific research.
