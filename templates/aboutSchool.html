About HB Kapadia School

Founded in 1952 by the visionary educationist Mr. Haribhai Kalyandas Kapadia, our school has grown from humble beginnings to become one of Gujarat's premier educational institutions. With over seven decades of excellence, we continue to nurture young minds and shape future leaders.

Our institution stands as a testament to Mr. Kapadia's vision of holistic education that combines academic rigor with character development. Today, we serve students across multiple campuses, maintaining the highest standards of education while staying true to our founding principles.

We believe in fostering an environment where students can explore their talents, develop critical thinking skills, and grow into responsible citizens. Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to ensure each student receives personalized attention and guidance.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to become responsible global citizens and lifelong learners.

  • Foster intellectual curiosity and critical thinking
  • Promote ethical values and social responsibility
  • Encourage creativity and innovation
  • Develop leadership qualities and teamwork skills

Our Vision

To be a beacon of educational excellence that inspires generations of learners to achieve their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

  • Academic excellence through innovative teaching methods
  • Holistic development of mind, body, and spirit
  • Inclusive environment that celebrates diversity
  • Sustainable practices for environmental stewardship

Our Founder's Legacy

Mr. Haribhai Kalyandas Kapadia established our school with a vision to provide quality education rooted in Indian values while embracing global perspectives. His philosophy continues to guide our educational approach today.

The founder believed that education should not merely impart knowledge but also shape character. This principle remains at the heart of our institution, influencing everything from our curriculum design to our extracurricular activities.

Under his leadership, the school laid strong foundations that have enabled successive generations to build upon his vision. Today, we honor his legacy by continuing to strive for excellence in all our endeavors.
