School Activities

Enriching Student Life Through Diverse Extracurricular Programs

At Kapadia High School, we believe in holistic education that extends beyond the classroom. Our diverse range of activities are designed to nurture talents, build character, and create well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of tomorrow.

Through our carefully curated extracurricular programs, students discover new interests, develop essential life skills, and create lasting memories that shape their educational journey.

Sports Activities

Physical fitness and sportsmanship are integral parts of our educational philosophy.

Cricket

Our cricket team participates in inter-school tournaments and has won several district-level championships.

Basketball

Our state-of-the-art basketball court hosts regular practice sessions and competitive matches.

Athletics

Our annual sports meet showcases the athletic talents of our students in various track and field events.

Cultural Activities

We encourage students to explore and express their creativity through various cultural programs.

Dance

Our dance troupe performs various classical and contemporary dance forms at school functions and competitions.

Music

Students learn vocal and instrumental music under the guidance of experienced teachers.

Drama

Our drama club stages plays and skits that showcase the acting talents of our students.

Academic Activities

We organize various academic activities to enhance learning beyond the classroom.

Science Club

Our science club conducts experiments, organizes science fairs, and participates in science competitions.

Debate Club

Students develop public speaking skills and critical thinking through regular debate sessions.

Robotics Club

Students learn to design, build, and program robots, preparing them for the technological future.

Annual Events

Our school calendar is filled with exciting events throughout the academic year.

Annual Day

A grand celebration showcasing the talents of our students through various cultural performances.

Sports Day

A day dedicated to athletic competitions, showcasing the sporting talents of our students.

Science Exhibition

Students showcase their scientific knowledge through innovative projects and models.
